The Driver's App is designed to streamline the management of delivery orders. It allows you to assign orders to yourself, update their status, and confirm when they have been delivered. This article will guide you through using the Driver's App efficiently, step by step.

Step 1: Download the Driver app

The first thing you need to do is download the Driver app. You can find it here, for both Android and iOS.

Step 2: Log in to the app and set your availability

After you download the app, you can log in with your credentials. Then, you will be able to change your activity status to the one available in the Driver status section.

Step 3: View orders to be fulfilled

Go to the Driver tab, and you can start the course immediately if you have assigned orders. If there are unassigned orders, you can assign them to yourself. This depends on how your restaurant manages the assignment of orders to drivers. You can read more about it here: Managing your own drivers & deliveries.

Step 4: Start your delivery trip

If you have an order assigned to you, you can click on the Start the trip button. You can use navigation apps such as Google Maps or Apple Maps to find the desired address.

Open the details of a given order and plan your route using the map.

Step 5: Let the customer know you are there (when you arrive)

The customer will not know that you have arrived with his order if you do not inform him. Remember to have a way to contact the recipient of the order.

Step 6: Collect payment from the customer (when the order is not paid online)

Remember to collect the payment from the customer if the order was not paid online. You can check whether the order was paid online in the Driver tab.

Step 7: Mark order as delivered (or not delivered if there was a problem)

Once you deliver the order to the customer, mark it accordingly. If, for some reason, you weren't able to deliver the order, please make sure to mark that in the app as well.

Step 8: Finish the delivery trip

Once your order has been delivered and marked in the app, click the Finish trip button to end your delivery trip.


How to disable the Driver App?

In order to disable the Driver App, you will have to deactivate the appropriate user in settings (the one with Driver permissions). Also, you will have to turn off the service from the Billing, in this tab.
