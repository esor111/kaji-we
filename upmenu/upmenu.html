About us

We create online systems for gastronomy that help increase sales and build your community of happy and loyal customers. Through the constant development of our tools, their simplicity and speed of implementation, we want each restaurant to be able to operate online independently, on its own terms, without the participation of external portals for ordering food. We offer our services in the subscription model. Depending on your needs, you can choose from three plans. What can we do together? - Create a website that will allow you to save on commission paid to external portals; - Configure the menu that will arouse the appetite of your guests; - Set up orders and delivery zones to efficiently reach your target group; - Launch advanced marketing campaigns, incl. coupons, promotions, newsletters, PUSH and SMS campaigns to help you build an engaged community; - Reward your guests in the loyalty program so that they feel your restaurant is something more than just another gastronomic point; Launch your own mobile application, thanks to which ordering food will be even faster, easier and more convenient for your guests. We want to help you achieve your business goals and build a long-term partnership relationship from which we will derive tangible benefits together, which is why we provide support at every stage of cooperation.
