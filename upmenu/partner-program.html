Choose Your Partnership Model and Start Earning

At UpMenu, we offer three types of partner programs – choose the one that best
fits your needs.

Affiliate (referral program)

Become a Partner


Recommend UpMenu and earn a 16% recurring commission for as long as your client uses the system and pays for their subscription.

  • We handle all billing — you focus on promotion and recommending UpMenu.
  • Customer support is on our side — no need for detailed product knowledge.
  • The simplest partnership model — passive income.

For whom:

  • If you’re looking for a simple model without spending time on sales or support — recommended for most partners.

    Reseller

    Become a Partner


    Buy from us with a 16.5% – 50% reseller discount, set your own prices, and sell to your clients on your own terms.

    • You invoice your clients directly — earn the margin between your UpMenu purchase price and your selling price.
    • You build direct relationships with your clients — customer support is handled on your side
    • Flexibility – combine UpMenu with your own services at any price (e.g., hardware sales or consulting).

    For whom:

    • If you want to build direct relationships with clients, invoice them yourself, and sell UpMenu on your own terms — combined with your own services or products.

    Reseller White Label

    Become a Partner


    Sell the same way as a reseller, but under your own brand — restaurants won’t see the UpMenu brand.

    • Full control over branding – clients see your brand, not UpMenu.
    • Your own product without the need to build technology from scratch.
    • Grow your business and brand — position yourself as a provider of complete tech solutions for the restaurant industry.

    For whom:

    • If you’re an entrepreneur who wants to build your own brand, have full control over branding, pricing, and positioning — as a technology provider for restaurants.

    4 questions to help choose a partnership model

    • Do I want to provide support to the restaurants? (If not, choose affiliate)
    • Do I want to invoice my clients myself? (If yes, choose reseller)
    • Do I need to set my own pricing and prices? (If yes, choose reseller)
    • Do I want the restaurants not to see the UpMenu brand? (If yes, choose white-label reseller)

      Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

      What is UpMenu?

      UpMenu is an all-in-one restaurant platform that partners can resell or refer. It covers sales, operations management, and guest engagement—so your clients grow while you build predictable recurring revenue under your brand.

      What makes UpMenu different from competitors?

      • True white-label & pricing control: sell under your brand and keep the margin.

      • All-in-one stack: one platform to pitch, deploy, and support—less vendor sprawl.

      • Direct channel value prop: clients own customers & data (no marketplace fees), making the sale easier.

      • Partner enablement: 1-on-1 onboarding, sales decks, demos, and a dedicated success manager.

      • Integrations & APIs: works with leading POS, KDS, and payment providers for smoother deployments.

      • Recurring revenue model: transparent economics designed for long-term partner growth.

      Do you provide support for resellers?

      Yes. We provide ready-to-use sales/tech materials, 1-on-1 help with initial setup, and ongoing support whenever issues arise.

      We’re always available to chat and you’ll have a dedicated Partner Success Manager to help you win deals and keep clients happy.

      Do I need to have a registered business to become a partner?

      • Reseller / White Label: Yes. You’ll be buying subscriptions from UpMenu and reselling them to your clients, so you must be able to invoice, collect/pay taxes, and run billing—which requires a registered business in most countries.
      • Affiliate: Not required. Individuals or companies can join. You’ll receive commissions and are responsible for any local tax reporting.

      Who provides customer support for restaurants?

      • Reseller: UpMenu’s in-app chat support is available to your customers. They can contact us directly or you, depending on preference. Chat is English-only—if your market needs another language, consider offering local live support.

      • White-Label Reseller: You provide all support. UpMenu chat is disabled for your customers so the experience remains fully on your brand.

      • Affiliate: UpMenu handles onboarding and ongoing product support for all customers you refer. You’re welcome to stay involved, but no support is required from you.
