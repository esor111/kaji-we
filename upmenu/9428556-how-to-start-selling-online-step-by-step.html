How to start selling online? (Step-by-Step)

Learn how to start selling online with the UpMenu online food ordering system directly from your restaurant website.

D
Written by Dominik Bartoszek
Updated over a week ago
Table of contents

This guide is a roadmap to seamlessly transition your restaurant into a digital platform. It covers crucial steps such as setting up your address and contact details, defining delivery zones, crafting your menu, and installing the UpMenu online ordering system.

Follow the steps below for comprehensive instructions and expert tips to kickstart your online sales journey effectively.

Leave it to us

If you don't have time or technical knowledge, let us set up your restaurant.

Order: Free Setup Service

Step 1: Enter name & address

Provide all necessary restaurant details.

  1. Login to your Dashboard.

  2. Click on the Start selling online button.

  3. Enter your restaurant name, address & contact details in the Settings ‚Üí Address and contact tab.

  4. Save changes by clicking on the Save button.

Step 2: Select order types

Determine how you want to fulfill customers' orders, such as deliveries, pickups, dine-in, or create your own custom methods.

  1. Go to Online ordering ‚Üí Fulfillment methods.

  2. Select your order fulfillment methods.

  3. Click on Advanced settings to add a new custom delivery type. (optional)

  4. Save changes by clicking on the Save button.

Step 3: Select a menu template

Choose your menu template to display your menu visually appealing.

  1. Go to Shop settings ‚Üí Template.

  2. Choose a menu template that fits your branding.

  3. Click on the Preview button to see how your menu will look on your restaurant website.

  4. Click the Activate button to choose a template.

  5. Confirm changes by clicking on the Change template button.

Learn more

  • Choosing a template

Step 4: Add your menu

Uploading your online menu is an essential step to start offering your menu products online.

  1. Go to Menu.

  2. Import your menu from a file or a link.

  3. Select upload a file with the menu or provide a link.

  4. Wait a few minutes to finish importing your menu.

  5. Check if the menu is imported properly.

  6. Save import by clicking on the Accept and save button.

Other methods to add a menu

  • Create a menu manually - learn more here how to create a menu

  • Order a free setup service, and our team will handle the rest!

Step 5: Add delivery zones

Delivery zones allow your customers to check for what locations your restaurant delivers and for what price.

  1. Go to Online ordering ‚Üí Delivery zones.

  2. Click on the Add zone button.

  3. Zoom out your map.

  4. Click on the map to create a starting point and drag it through the map.

  5. Enter the name of your delivery zone.

  6. Provide the amount for a delivery fee. (optional)

  7. Save changes by clicking on the Save button.

Learn more

  • Setting up delivery zones

  • Setting delivery hours for a specific delivery zone

Step 6: Add an ordering button to a website

  1. Go to Online ordering ‚Üí Installation.

  2. Select the option Add ‚ÄúOrder now‚Äù button to your existing website.

  3. Enter the website address where you want to add the "Order now" button and click the Next button.

  4. Copy the website address from UpMenu and click on the Save button to save changes.

  5. Go to your restaurant website's navigation and add a button to the navigation bar (e.g., 'Order Now' or 'Order Online') with a link you copied in UpMenu - a link to your online ordering page.

  6. Check the installation status in Online ordering ‚Üí Installation. If you properly added a button with a link on your restaurant website's navigation, you should see the following status: Installation successful.
