Mobile app for ordering presentation

Find out how our mobile app for ordering looks in practice!

If you use an Android device, you can try our mobile app for ordering completely free. In the case of an iOS device, we recommend you download a demonstrative version of our app. You can find it by simply searching for ‚ÄúUpMenu‚Äù in Apple Store.

  1. Go to ‚ÄúMobile app‚Äù.
