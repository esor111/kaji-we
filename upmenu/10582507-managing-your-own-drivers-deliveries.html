3rd party delivery services vs own deliveries and drivers

If your restaurant employs in-house drivers (a dedicated courier fleet), this module allows you to efficiently manage the entire delivery process.

If you don‚Äôt have in-house drivers, you can still offer delivery by using third-party delivery services. In this case, instead of using the delivery management module, you would use the Delivery Dispatch module, which allows you to assign deliveries to external providers such as Uber Direct, Glovo On Demand, or Wolt Drive.

Here, you can find a step-by-step guide on how to set up and use this module.

Step 1: Enable the Delivery Management Module

Step 2: Add Driver accounts (Module Configuration)

Add users with the Driver permission. The system will automatically activate the delivery management module, allowing you to manage deliveries.

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Users and click on New member.

  2. Enter the e-mail address, provide the proper data, and set the password.

  3. In the Permissions for modules section, choose Drivers ‚Üí Access to be the driver.

  4. Click Save.

Read more about how to create and manage users in general.

Step 3: Decide how you want to manage deliveries

There are two main ways to assign orders to drivers:

  1. Dispatcher-Assigned Orders ‚Äì The restaurant staff (dispatcher) manually assigns orders to specific drivers, deciding who will handle each delivery.

  2. Driver-Claimed Orders (Order Pool) ‚Äì Instead of assigning orders, staff simply notify drivers when new deliveries are available. Drivers then select and claim orders themselves.

Step 4: Assign orders to Drivers

If you manually assign orders to drivers:

  1. After accepting an order, go to the Delivery Map, click Assign Driver, and then select Mark as Ready for Pickup.

  2. The order will be assigned to the selected driver and will appear in the Driver App for fulfillment.

If drivers claim orders themselves (Order Pool):

  1. After accepting an order, go to the Delivery Map and click Mark as Ready for Pickup (do not assign the order to a specific driver).

  2. The order will appear in the Driver App under the order pool, allowing drivers to self-assign it.

Step 5: Order fulfillment by the Driver (using the Driver App)

Drivers download the Driver App (available on Android or iOS) and use it to manage and fulfill deliveries.

Additionally, the Driver App tracks driver locations in real time, allowing restaurant staff to monitor all active deliveries and driver movements. Customers can also track their orders and see the courier‚Äôs location for a seamless delivery experience.
