How to enable table reservations? (Step-by-Step)

In this article, you‚Äôll learn how to enable table reservations in your restaurant. This will allow your customers to reserve tables at any time of the day through your restaurant‚Äôs website or mobile app.

Step 1: Enable table reservations

  1. Log in to your Dashboard.

  2. Go to Reservations.

  3. Click on the toggle button to enable or disable table reservations.

  4. Specify the date and time of reservation availability.

  5. Specify the minimum and the maximum number of people per reservation.

  6. Click on the Save button.

Step 2: Add table reservations to your website

  1. Log in to your Dashboard.

  2. Go to Pages.

  3. Click Add page and add a blank page.

  4. Name your new page (e.g., Reservations).

  5. Go to Edit Website.

  6. Select the newly created page from the drop-down menu at the top of the screen.

  7. On that page, click Add a block and select the 1/1 block.

  8. Once the block has been created, click on the + icon and add the Booking widget.

  9. You may click the pencil icon to edit your widget.

  10. Make sure to save the changes by clicking the Save changes button in the top right corner of the screen.
