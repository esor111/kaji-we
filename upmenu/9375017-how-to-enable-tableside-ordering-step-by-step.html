Table of contents

Tableside ordering revolutionizes the dining experience, empowering restaurants to seamlessly take orders directly at the table, enhancing customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

This article explains what tableside ordering is for restaurants and guides you through the simple steps to enable this feature with UpMenu, streamlining your service and delighting your customers.

What is a tableside ordering?

Tableside ordering refers to allowing restaurant staff to take orders directly from customers at their table, typically using a handheld device like a tablet or smartphone.

Nowadays, tableside ordering usually means using digital systems where customers use their phones to place orders.

Enabling a tableside ordering

Step 1: Add your menu

  1. Login to your Dashboard.

  2. Go to Menu.

Learn more about creating a menu here: How to create a menu?

Step 2: Design a QR Code

  1. Login to your Dashboard.

  2. Go to QR Code Menu & Tableside ordering.

  3. Choose a QR code template.

  4. Select a background color and text color.

  5. If you want to show your restaurant logo on the QR code, check the option Display brand logo.

Step 3: Activate table ordering

  1. Check Enable tableside ordering button to activate table ordering.

  2. Save changes by clicking on the Save button.

  3. To download QR codes, click the Download button.
